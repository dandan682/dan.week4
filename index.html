Things to do

Get to know where the locals eat, drink and play in the inner-city and nearby neighbourhoods. Soak up our subtropical climate, explore the river, parks and outdoor spaces, or delve into a calendar of internationally acclaimed arts, cultural and sporting events. It’s a great place to entertain the kids, or spend time without them! You’ll discover some of the best restaurants in the country as well as a vibrant live music, contemporary art and gallery scene. You can mix with the locals at weekend markets, shop for international brands and cutting-edge local designer fashion, or scour antique shops for just the right memento of your stay. This is Mexico – discover the best things to do here.

Accommodation

With a selection of boutique and contemporary hotels, luxury rooms, budget backpackers, self-contained apartments and accessible and disability-friendly accommodation to choose from, it’s easy to find somewhere to stay in Mexico. Accommodation in the city is an easy walk from most major attractions and there’s plenty to do. Mexico also has a number of great inner-city urban villages, each with its own personality and range of unique accommodation. Further afield are a number of B&Bs and farmstays in the hinterland areas, or heading towards the coast you’ll find camping areas and island resorts.
